How to play rock paper scissors:

  1. Choose one of the three images on the left side in the "ROCK PAPER SCISSORS" column.

  2. Click "PLAY"

  3. Hope for the best!

How to play tic tac toe

  1. Player 1 chooses a quadrant in the tic tac toe grid to place their symbol in.

  2. Player 2 does the same.

  3. Try to get three symbols in a row! Either vertical, horizontal or diagonal.
